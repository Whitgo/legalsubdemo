# LegalSub

> **A boutique subpoena & Release of Information (ROI) platform built for healthcare, law enforcement agencies, and banking — delivering faster, more accurate results than the competition.**

---

## 🚀 What is LegalSub?

LegalSub is a modern, purpose-built platform that streamlines the entire subpoena and ROI workflow for organizations that operate in high-compliance, time-sensitive legal environments.

Whether you're a hospital compliance team, a law enforcement agency, or a financial institution, LegalSub replaces slow, error-prone manual processes with a secure, automated, and auditable system.

---

## 🏥 Who It's For

| Sector | Use Case |
|---|---|
| **Healthcare** | HIPAA-compliant medical record requests, ROI processing, court-ordered disclosures |
| **Law Enforcement** | Subpoena response tracking, inter-agency record sharing, chain-of-custody documentation |
| **Banking & Finance** | Legal hold management, subpoena compliance, financial record disclosures |

---

## ✨ Key Features

- ⚡ **Faster Processing** — Automated intake and routing cuts response times dramatically vs. legacy systems
- 🔒 **Secure & Compliant** — Built with HIPAA, GLBA, and law enforcement data standards in mind
- 📋 **End-to-End Tracking** — Full audit trail from request intake to fulfillment
- 📄 **Smart Document Handling** — Structured document ingestion, review, and secure delivery
- 🔔 **Automated Notifications** — Keep requestors and internal teams updated at every stage
- 📊 **Reporting & Analytics** — Track volumes, turnaround times, and compliance metrics

## 🖥️ Tech Stack
